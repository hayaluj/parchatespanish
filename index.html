About Parchate

More than Spanish —
it's a vibe.

"Parchate" is Colombian slang for "hang out with us." That's exactly what learning here feels like — relaxed, fun, and deeply real.

We are a collective of passionate Spanish teachers from Medellín, a city known for its warm people and melodic Spanish — and that authenticity drives everything we do.

Real language learning happens beyond the classroom too. That is why we organize events around Medellín, taking our students to hidden gems across the city — from local markets to cultural spots most tourists never discover — to practice Spanish in authentic, everyday settings.

Whether you're planning to visit Medellín, work with Colombian clients, or just fell in love with vallenato — we've got you. Our method focuses on conversation, confidence, and cultural fluency from day one.

🌺 Founded by Colombians from Medellín, for anyone who wants to connect with Colombia.
